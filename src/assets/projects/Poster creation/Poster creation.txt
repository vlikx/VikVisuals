Project: Visual Communication – Message-Driven Poster Design

- The Vision: Creating high-impact visual narratives that communicate urgent social messages and allow for deep personal interpretation.
- Creative Process: Developed in my 2nd semester, starting with collaborative brainstorming sessions to pitch concepts to professors for final approval.
- Production: Conducted professional photo shoots in the university studio; for larger-scale setups, I worked in the studio of Michael Ankenbrand, who provided technical guidance.
- The "Doomscrolling" Piece: A targeted critique of modern social media consumption, highlighting the psychological "trap" affecting today’s youth through digital distortion.
- The "Vertieft" Piece: Designed as an open-ended visual metaphor, using a spiral motif to represent being "deeply immersed" in a situation, leaving the emotional context (positive or negative) to the viewer's current life stage.
- Tech Stack: Expertly combined Photoshop for complex image manipulation and compositing with InDesign for precise layout and typography.
